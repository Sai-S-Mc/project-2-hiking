Hiking is a rewarding hobby for people of all ages and fitness levels. It's a great way to enjoy the outdoors, discover new places, and stay active. Whether you're taking a short walk through a local trail or tackling a challenging mountain path, hiking offers both physical and mental benefits. It's also a chance to unplug, clear your mind, and connect with nature at your own pace.
